Welcome!

A Mason is a man and a brother whose trust is in God. He meets you on the level and acts upon the square. truth is his compass and he is ever plumb. He has a true grip on all that is rite. He is loyal to his order and whatever his degree he is master of himself. In the Lodge of Life he wears unstained the white lambskin of Innocence. From his initiation as an Entered Apprentice he travels ever east toward the Light of wisdom until he receives the final divine password that admits him into the ineffable presence of the eternal supreme Grand Master of the universe: GOD.
